My hobby is

Snowboarding

Since I was 7 years old, I had been involved in snow sports. It started initially with just loving when it snowed in the city, to taking ski trips. Firstly, I learned how to ski. After about 7 years of skiing, perfecting that skill, I had a deep interest in snowboarding. So, one day with the help of a snowboard instructor I learned the basics and I was eager to learn more.

Ever since then I haven’t stopped going snowboarding, making it a must every winter. As the years go by, I get more and more skilled and now I can go down any difficulty of slopes. One of my favorite places to go is Mavrovo because it’s a quick destination to go with friends and I always have fun even if there is not much snow. I’ve taken many trips with friends and family and will continue to do so.
